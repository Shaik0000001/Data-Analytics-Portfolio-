# Hypothesis Testing

## 🎯 Hypothesis
UPI payments lead to higher average order value than COD.

## 📊 Method
Two-sample T-test comparing UPI and COD transactions.

## 📈 Steps
1. Separate UPI and COD transactions
2. Calculate mean order value
3. Perform T-test

## 📉 Result
If p-value < 0.05 → statistically significant difference.

## ✅ Conclusion
UPI users tend to spend more than COD users.
