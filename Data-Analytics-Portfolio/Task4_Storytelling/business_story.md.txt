# Business Story

## 🎯 Objective
Analyze e-commerce data to improve customer retention and revenue.

## 🔍 Problem
Most customers purchase only once.

## 📊 Analysis
- Electronics category drives highest revenue
- Customers prefer digital payment methods

## 💡 Insight
High-value purchases but low repeat rate.

## ✅ Recommendation
- Introduce loyalty programs
- Offer discounts for repeat customers
- Promote digital payment incentives
