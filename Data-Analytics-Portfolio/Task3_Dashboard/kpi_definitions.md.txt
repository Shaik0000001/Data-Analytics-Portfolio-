# KPI Definitions

## 1. Total Revenue
Total revenue generated from all sales.

Formula:
Revenue = Price × Quantity

---

## 2. Average Order Value (AOV)
Average value of each order.

Formula:
AOV = Total Revenue / Number of Orders

---

## 3. Customer Count
Total number of unique customers.

---

## 4. Top Category
Category generating highest revenue.

---

## 5. Monthly Sales Growth
Measures revenue increase over time.
