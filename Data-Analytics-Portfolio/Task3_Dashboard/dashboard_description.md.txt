# Dashboard Description

## 🎯 Objective
To provide an interactive view of sales performance and customer behavior.

## 📊 Dashboard Components

1. Revenue by Category (Bar Chart)
2. Monthly Sales Trend (Line Chart)
3. Top Products (Table)
4. Customer Distribution (Pie Chart)

## 🔍 Filters
- Date
- City
- Category

## 🛠 Tool Used
Power BI

## 💡 Purpose
Helps stakeholders identify trends, top-performing products, and customer patterns.
